ACM Student Chapter

The Chitkara University ACM Student Chapter is an official student body Chartered on 28 February 2010. This Chapter aims at creating a technomotive environment for the students of the Computer Science field apart from the regular course content. The chapter endeavours to amplify the practical expertise of its members by arranging various resourceful workshops, expert-talks, industry-oriented courses, hands-on workshops, industrial visits, quizzes and coding competitions. The forte of the chapter is to create an aura of the technical field, providing prerequisite knowledge to our members to provide them with an exposure to the competitive computing world. It is the dedication and corporation of our team along with a constant hunger for perfection, striving it to be the best chapter.
